# The Corrupted Guard

Former members of the Veil Guard who have broken from the organization and now operate with opposing or destabilizing goals.

---

## Overview

The Corrupted Guard is the name used by the Veil Guard and spy network for former Veil Guard operatives who have gone rogue. They are not a unified organization — they are a collection of individuals and cells, connected by their shared break from the Veil Guard and, in many cases, by ideological alignment with the Inversion or with the Betrayer's goals.

"Corrupted" is a Veil Guard designation and carries that faction's framing. From the inside, many members of the Corrupted Guard would describe themselves as the only honest actors in a system that has already failed.

---

## Origins

The Corrupted Guard emerged from within the Veil Guard following the Betrayal. The fracture points:

- Some Veil Guard members believed the Betrayer had justifiable reasons — the covenant itself was flawed
- Others were radicalized by the Inversion's acceleration — if the system is failing anyway, why defend it?
- Some were corrupted more literally — direct contact with Inversion energy or Shadewoven entities altered their perspective and alignment

---

## Structure and Operations

The Corrupted Guard has no central command. It operates in independent cells that:

- Actively work to prevent Veil reinforcement operations
- Recruit shadebound individuals and lost soul anomalies as assets
- In some cases, actively accelerate the Inversion (though motivations vary — some believe this leads to a better outcome; some are simply pursuing power)
- Maintain former Veil Guard contacts as inside sources

---

## Ideological Diversity

Not all Corrupted Guard members share the same goals:

| Faction within Faction | Position |
|------------------------|----------|
| Inversion Accelerants | Believe the Inversion is necessary or inevitable; help it along |
| Covenant Abolitionists | Oppose the Protector system entirely; want a new order |
| Opportunists | No ideology; exploiting the chaos for personal gain |
| Reluctant Defectors | Broke with the Veil Guard for specific reasons; not committed to larger destabilization |

---

## Threat Assessment

The Veil Guard considers the Corrupted Guard its primary operational threat in the Living World. The spy network shares this assessment. The Corrupted Guard's access to former Veil Guard training and protocols makes them unusually capable adversaries.
