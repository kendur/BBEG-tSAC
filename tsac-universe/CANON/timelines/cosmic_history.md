# Cosmic History

The history of the tSAC cosmos from its origins through the age before the Betrayal.

---

## The Primordial Era

*Before the current cosmic structure existed.*

The Primordial Era is the period before Brighthaven and Shadewoven were distinct realms. In this age, light and shadow were undifferentiated — neither structure nor dissolution had precedence. The Primordials are entities from this era; most are inert or dissolved, but their residue shapes the deep structure of the cosmos.

The transition from the Primordial Era to the current structure is referred to as **the Differentiation** — the moment when light and shadow separated into distinct principles and the three-realm structure emerged.

*Note: Details of the Differentiation are mythic and disputed. What follows is reconstructed from Protector-era records.*

---

## The Protector Covenant Era

*The period of stable cosmic governance.*

Following the Differentiation, the Protectors were appointed — or emerged — as stewards of the new structure. The Protector Covenant was established, governing:

- The maintenance of the Veil between planes
- The non-interference principle in mortal affairs
- The collective decision-making requirement for major actions
- The prohibition against claiming mortal souls

During this era:
- The Living World developed and diversified
- Mortal civilization emerged and built institutions
- The Veil Guard was established as a mortal institution aligned with the Protector mission
- Soul metaphysics developed to their current stable form

This era is estimated to span a very long period — possibly comparable to the total recorded span of mortal history several times over.

---

## The Pre-Betrayal Age

*The period immediately before the Betrayal — signs of strain.*

Protector records (fragmentary, partially suppressed) suggest that the era before the Betrayal was not as stable as the long Covenant Era. Key indicators:

- Disputes within the Protector collective about covenant interpretation
- Increasing Veil instability in certain regions
- Emergence of the Corrupted Guard precursors — Veil Guard members questioning the covenant
- Unusual soul anomalies not fully explained by existing mechanics

Whether these were causes or symptoms of the Betrayal is a significant historical debate.

---

## The Betrayal

*The pivotal event that ended the Covenant Era.*

The Betrayer — a shadow-aligned Protector — took unilateral action that violated the covenant and destabilized the Veil. The specific nature of that action is not yet canon. The Inversion began at this moment.

The other Protectors did not prevent it. Whether this was inability, complicity, or surprise is unknown.
