# Current Age

The present era of the tSAC story — the Inversion Age.

---

## Overview

The Current Age is the period following the Betrayal in which the Inversion is actively progressing. It is the era in which the tSAC story takes place. The defining characteristic of this age is **uncertainty**: institutions that have operated for centuries are straining; the cosmic balance is visibly deteriorating; and the agents capable of intervening are fractured by conflicting interests.

---

## Key Markers of the Current Age

The following are confirmed developments of the current era:

| Marker | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Inversion Progression | Light and shadow are bleeding across the Veil; rate is increasing |
| Lost Soul Surge | Lost soul incidents are far above historical baseline |
| Veil Guard Fracture | The Corrupted Guard split has weakened institutional Veil maintenance |
| Shadebound Rise | New shadebound cases appearing at elevated frequency |
| Solas Aghrian Active | The spiritlocked mortal has entered the active conflict |
| Betrayer at Large | The Betrayer is present and acting in the Living World |

---

## Factions in the Current Age

The current political/factional landscape:

- **Veil Guard** — Strained, understaffed, increasingly authoritarian in its methods as panic sets in
- **Spy Network** — Information-rich but action-constrained; watching the situation deteriorate with frustration
- **Corrupted Guard** — Opportunistic and ideologically diverse; capitalizing on Veil Guard weakness
- **Remaining Protectors** — Present but not openly intervening; their constraints and motivations are unclear to mortals

---

## Story Present

The story of tSAC begins at a specific point in the Current Age:

- The Inversion is visibly accelerating — no longer deniable even by institutions that wanted to keep it quiet
- Solas Aghrian has recently come to the attention of both the Veil Guard and the spy network
- The Betrayer has made their first direct contact with a mortal (details to be developed)
- A major Veil anomaly event is imminent (details to be developed)

---

## Open Questions (Current Canon Gaps)

The following are known unknowns — acknowledged gaps in current canon that will be filled through story development:

- The specific geography of the story's primary setting
- The political structure of the mortal government in the primary setting
- The exact nature of the first Betrayer/mortal contact
- The timeline from the story's opening to the major Veil anomaly event
