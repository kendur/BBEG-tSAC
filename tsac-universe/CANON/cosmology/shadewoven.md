# Shadewoven

The realm of shadow, memory, and dissolution.

---

## Nature

Shadewoven is the cosmic plane associated with potential, entropy, and the unformed. It is not darkness in the sense of evil — it is darkness in the sense of the space before definition, the silence before sound, the canvas before painting. It holds everything that has been forgotten, everything that might yet be, and everything that has dissolved back into potential.

In Shadewoven, nothing is lost entirely. What fades from mortal memory does not disappear — it enters the shadow-weave and persists in a state of dissolved potential. This makes Shadewoven a realm of immense stored power, and immense danger.

---

## Appearance

Accounts of Shadewoven describe:

- A landscape that shifts and reforms, never quite settling into permanent shape
- Deep colors — indigo, black, silver, deep violet — that seem to have depth beyond physical color
- The sensation of hearing whispers of things once known and forgotten
- A weight of potential, as though the air itself is full of things waiting to become

---

## Inhabitants

Shadewoven is home to:

- **Protectors of Shadow** — Protectors aligned with the Shadewoven principle
- **Memory-wraiths** — dissolved soul-fragments that have not fully integrated into the weave
- **The Woven** — ancient semi-conscious entities that are neither soul nor construct, but pattern

---

## Relationship to the Living World

Shadow bleeds into the Living World as intuition, creativity, adaptability, and grief. Communities with strong Shadewoven influence tend toward flexibility and innovation, but also impermanence and collective memory loss.

Shadebound individuals — mortals carrying a fragment of the shadow-weave within them — serve as living conduits between Shadewoven and the Living World.

---

## The Inversion Threat

The Inversion risks flooding Shadewoven with light energy, which would impose false structure on what is meant to remain fluid. An over-structured Shadewoven would freeze memory-wraiths into trapped forms and potentially prevent dissolved souls from completing their natural cycle.
