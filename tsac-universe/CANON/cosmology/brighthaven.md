# Brighthaven

The realm of light, structure, and ordered existence.

---

## Nature

Brighthaven is the cosmic plane associated with clarity, permanence, and form. It is the source of what mortals perceive as divine light — not warmth or comfort necessarily, but the principle of things having definite shape, definite meaning, and enduring consequence.

In Brighthaven, nothing is forgotten. Every action taken in the Living World leaves an imprint here. This makes it a realm of absolute record — and, in its extreme, a realm of absolute judgment.

---

## Appearance

Those who have glimpsed Brighthaven — in visions, spiritlock encounters, or near-death states — describe it as:

- Vast, geometric spaces of white and gold light
- Architecture that seems to have grown rather than been built, with crystalline precision
- A silence that is not absence of sound but the absence of uncertainty
- A feeling of being known completely, which is either peace or terror depending on one's relationship to one's own actions

---

## Inhabitants

Brighthaven is home to:

- **Protectors of Light** — those Protectors aligned primarily with the Brighthaven principle
- **Soul-echoes** — the imprints of mortal souls who have passed and been absorbed into the record
- **Clarity Constructs** — non-sentient structures of pure metaphysical light that maintain the plane's order

---

## Relationship to the Living World

Light bleeds into the Living World as inspiration, pattern-recognition, moral certainty, and (when in excess) rigidity and intolerance. Communities with strong Brighthaven influence tend toward order, tradition, and law — for good and ill.

---

## The Inversion Threat

The Inversion risks flooding Brighthaven with shadow energy, which would destabilize the absolute-record nature of the plane. If Brighthaven becomes uncertain, the consequences for mortal memory, soul structure, and the nature of consequence itself could be catastrophic.
