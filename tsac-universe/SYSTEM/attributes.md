# Attributes

Character attributes and their meaning in the tSAC system.

---

## Overview

Attributes define a character's fundamental capabilities. They are deliberately few — the system prioritizes depth of interaction between a small number of attributes over breadth of granular modifiers.

---

## The Core Attributes

| Attribute | Description | Associated With |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Strength** | Physical power, endurance, and force application | Stamina pools, physical actions |
| **Agility** | Speed, precision, and physical adaptability | Defense, ranged actions, quick reactions |
| **Wit** | Intelligence, pattern-recognition, and problem-solving | Knowledge checks, tactical planning |
| **Resolve** | Mental endurance, willpower, and emotional stability | Willpower pools, resistance to supernatural influence |
| **Presence** | Social force, charisma, and ability to affect others | Persuasion, intimidation, leadership |
| **Attunement** | Sensitivity to spiritual/metaphysical forces | Interacting with Veil phenomena, detecting shadebound activity, spiritlock effects |

---

## Attribute Ratings

Attributes are rated on a scale of 1–5:

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Below average for the population |
| 2 | Average |
| 3 | Notably capable |
| 4 | Exceptional |
| 5 | Among the best possible for a mortal |

Most starting characters have primarily 2s and 3s, with one or two 4s reflecting specialization.

---

## Attunement as a Special Case

Attunement requires additional note. Unlike other attributes, Attunement interacts with:

- Spiritlock phenomena (characters with spiritlocks have modified Attunement interactions)
- Shadebound status (shadebound characters have Attunement complications — both bonuses and vulnerabilities)
- Veil proximity effects at spirit-thin places

High Attunement is not uniformly beneficial — it increases sensitivity to things that can harm as well as help.

---

## Derived Values

From the core attributes, several values are derived:

| Derived Value | Derivation |
|--------------|------------|
| Stamina (max) | Based on Strength + Resolve |
| Willpower (max) | Based on Resolve + Attunement |
| Defense Rating | Based on Agility + situational modifiers |
| Initiative | Based on Agility + Wit |
