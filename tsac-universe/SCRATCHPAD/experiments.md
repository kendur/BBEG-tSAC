# Experiments

Experimental concepts, alternative framings, and "what if" explorations. Nothing here is canon.

---

## Purpose

This file is a low-stakes space for:

- Alternative versions of canon elements that are being considered
- "What if" explorations of how the world might work differently
- Mechanical experiments with the game system
- Story structure alternatives to the current arc design

Experiments that prove out may be promoted to canon or story files. Experiments that don't work are kept here as a record of what was tried and why it didn't fit.
