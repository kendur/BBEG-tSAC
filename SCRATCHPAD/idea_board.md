# Incomplete Idea Board

Experimental concepts, alternative framings, and "what if" explorations. Nothing here is canon.

---

## Purpose

This file is a low-stakes space for:

- Alternative versions of canon elements that are being considered
- "What if" explorations of how the world might work differently
- Mechanical experiments with the game system
- Story structure alternatives to the current arc design

Experiments that prove out may be promoted to canon or story files. Experiments that don't work are kept here as a record of what was tried and why it didn't fit.

---

## Active Experiments

1. BRIGHTLOCK, SHADELOCK, SPIRITLOCK
2. update the AI Context to add AI context prompts to each file so that a human can use them for story, thematics, art or media generation, and to better align the users intent
3. fighting 2 ways
3.1 "follow your training" 
3.1.1 the player relinquishes some control of their characters combat choices 
3.1.2 allows the GM to use more exposition. 
3.1.3 player doesn't need to consider fighting moves if they don't enjoy our understand then 
3.1.4 GM handles most of the rolls unless the player wants to roll attacks 
3.2 "all in"
3.2.1 player calls
all the shots, moves, defenses, etc.
3.2.2 GM has less freedom to turn the fight into a 'scene' but makes the player more involved in the mechanics and specifics of character abilities and actions 
follow your training is useful when they're are large numbers of participants in the fight or when the story is more important than the fight or the fight is trivial 

if you have time and the number of fighters is small, called shots gives the player much more agency and can allow them to focus on weak armor points, feints, distractions, etc
