# Souls

The nature and mechanics of souls in the tSAC universe.

---

## What Is a Soul

In tSAC, a soul is the metaphysical structure that gives a living being coherent identity across time. It is not purely a spiritual abstraction — it is a functional structure that:

- Anchors a being's experiences into a continuous self
- Interfaces with both Brighthaven (through the clarity of defined identity) and Shadewoven (through the depth of accumulated experience and memory)
- Determines what happens to a being after death

Souls are produced by the Living World through the interaction of light and shadow. They do not pre-exist birth and are not assigned from either cosmic plane — they emerge.

---

## Soul Structure

A soul has two primary components:

| Component | Description | Plane Connection |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Form** | The defined shape of identity — what makes this being distinct | Brighthaven |
| **Depth** | The accumulated experience, memory, and potential — the history of the self | Shadewoven |

Health of a soul requires both components in proportion. A soul with too much Form and not enough Depth becomes rigid and brittle. A soul with too much Depth and not enough Form loses coherent identity.

---

## Soul Lifecycle

1. **Emergence** — A soul forms at birth (or equivalent) through the light-shadow interaction of the Living World
2. **Life** — The soul accumulates experience, relationship, and consequence
3. **Death** — The physical anchor is removed; the soul begins to migrate toward its aligned plane
4. **Resolution** — The soul integrates into Brighthaven (as a soul-echo) or Shadewoven (as dissolved potential), or is disrupted into a lost soul

---

## Disruptions to the Soul Lifecycle

Several phenomena can interrupt the natural lifecycle:

- **Spiritlock** — The soul is bound and cannot migrate freely
- **Shadebound** — A fragment of Shadewoven becomes embedded in the soul, altering its balance
- **Lost Soul** — The migration fails; the soul drifts untethered between planes
- **Inversion effects** — The disrupted plane balance interferes with soul emergence and resolution
