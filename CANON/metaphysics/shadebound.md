# Shadebound

Mortals who carry a fragment of the Shadewoven within their soul.

---

## Definition

A shadebound individual is a mortal who has, through some event or lineage, become partially interpenetrated with the Shadewoven plane. A fragment of the shadow-weave is embedded in their soul structure, altering how they interact with both planes and the Veil.

---

## How Shadebound Condition Forms

The shadebound condition can arise from:

- **Veil proximity** — Extended time near a spirit-thin place where Shadewoven bleeds through
- **Direct shadow contact** — A direct encounter with a Shadewoven entity that leaves a residue
- **Inherited** — A parent who carried the condition may pass a fragment to a child (not guaranteed; mechanism unclear)
- **Deliberate binding** — A Protector of shadow alignment may create a shadebound individual intentionally

The Inversion is causing the shadebound condition to appear more frequently, as the weakened Veil creates more opportunities for shadow interpenetration.

---

## Effects of Being Shadebound

**Practical effects:**
- Heightened sensitivity to Shadewoven energy and entities
- Ability to perceive or interact with memory-wraiths and the unformed aspects of the shadow plane
- Partial resistance to certain forms of shadow-based harm (the shadow fragment acts as a buffer)

**Vulnerabilities:**
- The shadow fragment can be destabilized, causing soul structure damage
- Extreme exposure to Brighthaven energy can create painful conflict between the fragment and the rest of the soul
- Shadebound individuals attract attention from both Shadewoven entities and the Veil Guard

**Psychological effects:**
- A persistent awareness of dissolution and potential — things feel less permanently fixed than they do for unbound mortals
- Grief and memory carry unusual weight and persistence
- Creative and intuitive capacity is often heightened

---

## Shadebound and Factions

- **Veil Guard** — Views shadebound individuals as Veil security risks; monitors and sometimes recruits them
- **Spy Network** — Considers them valuable assets for Shadewoven-adjacent intelligence work
- **Corrupted Guard** — May actively recruit shadebound for less sanctioned purposes
