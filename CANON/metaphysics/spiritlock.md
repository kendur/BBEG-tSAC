# Spiritlock

The binding of a soul to a location, object, or person — preventing its natural migration.

---

## Definition

A spiritlock is a metaphysical binding that anchors a soul (or a portion of a soul) to a specific thing: a place, an object, or another living being. The binding prevents that soul from migrating to Brighthaven or Shadewoven after death, and may also affect the soul's behavior and experience during life.

Spiritlocks are not inherently good or evil — they are a phenomenon with a range of causes and consequences.

---

## How Spiritlocks Form

Spiritlocks can form through:

- **Deliberate binding** — A Protector or sufficiently powerful entity intentionally creates the lock
- **Traumatic attachment** — An event of extreme emotional or spiritual intensity creates an unintentional bond
- **Covenant-binding** — A formal promise or oath of sufficient metaphysical weight
- **Inherited lock** — A spiritlock can, in unusual circumstances, pass from one person to another across generations

Solas Aghrian's spiritlock is of the fourth type — inherited, and of unusual origin and power level.

---

## Effects of a Spiritlock

During life:
- The spiritlocked being has a heightened sensitivity to the Veil and to the thing they are bound to
- They may experience the memories or experiences of the soul-fragments bound alongside them
- Shadow and light energy interact with them differently than with unbound mortals

After death:
- The soul cannot migrate until the lock is resolved or broken
- An unresolved spiritlock after death typically produces a ghost-like entity or a lost soul depending on circumstances

---

## Breaking a Spiritlock

Spiritlocks can be broken, but doing so requires:

- Understanding the original cause of the binding
- Either resolving the underlying condition or using sufficient metaphysical force to sever it
- Accepting the consequences — breaking a lock releases the bound soul, which may have effects on the living world depending on the soul's state

---

## Solas Aghrian's Spiritlock

Solas's spiritlock is unusual in that it appears to bind them to Protector-level history. This is not a standard spiritlock origin. Its full nature is a plot mystery.
