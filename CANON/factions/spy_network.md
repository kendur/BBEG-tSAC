# The Spy Network

The mortal intelligence network operating in the Living World, focused on Veil-related threats and cosmic politics.

---

## Overview

The spy network is a mortal organization that monitors Veil-related activity, tracks the movements of significant metaphysical actors, and maintains intelligence on faction behavior. Unlike the Veil Guard, the spy network is not formally tied to the Protectors' covenant — it operates purely on mortal institutional interests.

The network's nominal purpose is the safety and stability of mortal civilization. In practice, it is entangled with power politics, competing faction interests, and the very destabilizing forces it monitors.

---

## Structure

The spy network is deliberately non-hierarchical to prevent single points of failure. It operates through:

- **Cells** — Small groups of operatives who know only their immediate colleagues
- **Handlers** — Mid-level coordinators who maintain cell connections and report upward
- **The Directorate** — A small senior body whose membership is not public even within the network
- **Assets** — Non-members who provide information or services without full membership

Loughan Begarrow is a field operative with unusual standing — they have connections across multiple cells and possibly to the Directorate level.

---

## Intelligence Focus Areas

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Spirit-thin places | Tracking destabilization events and unusual activity |
| Shadebound individuals | Monitoring those with shadow-interpenetration for signs of radicalization or exploitation |
| Faction movements | Intelligence on Veil Guard and Corrupted Guard activities |
| Protector sightings | Extremely rare; high-priority when they occur |
| Inversion indicators | Correlating anomalous events with Inversion progression models |

---

## Relationship to Other Factions

| Faction | Relationship |
|---------|-------------|
| Veil Guard | Information-sharing partnership with tension — the network is suspicious of Veil Guard's increasing autonomy |
| Corrupted Guard | Active intelligence target — primary threat assessment |
| Protectors | Network has standing orders to document any Protector contact; no direct relationship |

---

## The Network and Solas Aghrian

Loughan Begarrow's involvement with Solas is, from the network's perspective, an asset relationship. The network is aware that Solas's spiritlock gives them access to information and events that ordinary operatives cannot reach. Whether the network fully understands the nature of the spiritlock is unclear.
