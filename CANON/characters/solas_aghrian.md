# Solas Aghrian

The protagonist of the tSAC story.

---

## Overview

Solas Aghrian is a mortal who becomes entangled in the Protector-level conflict surrounding the Inversion. Their significance stems not from extraordinary ability or divine heritage, but from the spiritlock they carry — an ancient binding that connects them to events far beyond the scope of ordinary mortal life.

## The Spiritlock

Solas carries a spiritlock of unusual nature. Key confirmed facts:

- It predates Solas — they inherited or were bound to it, not born with it
- The spiritlock appears to connect to Protector-level events, which is highly irregular
- It has practical effects on how shadow and light energy interact with Solas
- It is both a source of capability and a source of vulnerability

## Narrative Role

Solas functions as the audience's point of entry into the cosmic conflict. They:

- Experience the Inversion's effects from the mortal ground level
- Are drawn into contact with Protectors, factions, and metaphysical entities
- Must make choices that have real consequences in both the Living World and the cosmic planes
- Embody the central thematic question: *what does an individual owe to a system larger than themselves?*
