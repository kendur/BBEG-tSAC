# The Living World

The mortal plane — the active interface between Brighthaven and Shadewoven.

---

## Nature

The Living World is where Brighthaven and Shadewoven interact. It is not a midpoint or a compromise — it is the dynamic product of their relationship. Life, change, meaning, and consequence all arise from the tension and interplay between light and shadow.

The Living World is uniquely characterized by **time** — a property that neither Brighthaven nor Shadewoven possesses in the same way. In Brighthaven, all moments are simultaneously recorded; in Shadewoven, moments dissolve into potential. Only in the Living World do moments pass, accumulate, and build into history.

---

## Geography and Structure

The Living World contains:

- **Physical geography** — continents, oceans, mountains, and the full range of natural environments
- **Spirit-thin places** — locations where the Veil is weak and the influence of Brighthaven or Shadewoven bleeds through strongly
- **The Veil itself** — not visible or physical, but perceptible to those with sensitivity to the planes

Spirit-thin places are significant locations for factions, magic, and plot. They are often sites of historical events where great spiritual energy was expended.

---

## Mortals and the Living World

Mortals are the primary inhabitants and, in many ways, the purpose of the Living World. They are the entities for whom change, consequence, and time have the most meaning.

Mortal souls are products of the Living World's light-shadow interaction. At death, a soul will naturally tend toward one of the planes depending on its nature — but this process can be disrupted by spiritlock, shadow-binding, or the effects of the Inversion.

---

## The Inversion's Impact on the Living World

The Inversion manifests in the Living World as:

- Increased frequency of spirit-thin place destabilizations
- Soul anomalies — shadebound individuals becoming more numerous and harder to stabilize
- Lost souls appearing in greater numbers
- Unusual weather and natural phenomena at cosmic nexus points
- Faction tensions rising as each group interprets the signs differently
