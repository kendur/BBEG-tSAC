# The Null

The Null are entities created directly by the Concordance.

They represent the system’s final response when equilibrium collapses beyond the control of the arbiters.

---

## Characteristics

- extremely rare
- not mortal
- pure manifestations of cosmic energy

At any given time there exist:

- one Brighthaven Null
- one Shadewoven Null

---

## Appearance

Null appear either:

- incomprehensibly horrifying
- impossibly beautiful

Their presence signals that equilibrium has failed.

Arbiters work to prevent situations requiring their intervention.
