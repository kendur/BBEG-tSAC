# Arbiters

Arbiters are individuals infused with both Brighthaven and Shadewoven energies.

They serve as agents of the Concordance and work to maintain equilibrium in the mortal world.

---

## Traits

Arbiters:

- do not age
- retain their mortal memories
- can still be destroyed
- possess enhanced perception of the ley-line lattice

---

## Arbiter Roles

### Examiners

Hidden observers who monitor patterns in the mortal world.

Rarely seen even by other arbiters.

---

### Inquisitors

Investigators who directly study disturbances and anomalies.

Most commonly encountered by mortals.

---

### Reclaimers

Retrieval specialists who recover dangerous artifacts, creatures, or spirits.

---

### Justicars

Champions who intervene during major imbalances.

Many historical heroes were secretly Justicars.

---

### Wardens

Guardians of vaults, relics, and Concordance archives.

They maintain containment structures for dangerous entities and artifacts.
