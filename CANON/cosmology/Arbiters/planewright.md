# Planewright

The Planewright is the coordinating intelligence of the Arbiter system.

At any given time, only **one Planewright exists**.

They act as the primary interpreter of disturbances within the **[Concordance](../concordance.md)**.

---

# Role

The Planewright does not command Arbiters in the traditional sense.

Instead, they observe large-scale equilibrium patterns across the **[ley-line lattice](../ley_lines.md)** and help guide Arbiter responses.

Responsibilities include:

- interpreting disturbances within the **[Concordance](../concordance.md)**
- coordinating Arbiter intervention
- maintaining the architecture of the Concordance layer
- preserving system stability

---

# Relationship to the Concordance

While all **[Arbiters](arbiters.md)** can perceive the Concordance, the Planewright has the deepest connection.

They are able to perceive structural patterns within the Concordance itself.

Over time, their presence subtly reshapes its architecture.

---

# Succession

A Planewright is replaced only when the current one is destroyed or relinquishes the role.

The **[Concordance](../concordance.md)** selects a successor from among the existing **[Arbiters](arbiters.md)**.

---

# Historical Significance

Some Planewrights leave profound marks upon the structure of the Concordance.

Their architectural influence may remain visible for centuries within the deeper layers of the system.

---

# Limitations

The Planewright does not possess unlimited authority.

Even they cannot directly alter the equilibrium between **[Brighthaven](../energies/brighthaven.md)** and **[Shadewoven](../energies/shadewoven.md)**.

They can only guide those who act within the system.

Attempting to force equilibrium rather than preserve it risks destabilizing the Concordance itself.
