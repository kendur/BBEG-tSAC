# The Concordance

The Concordance is the underlying equilibrium system governing the universe.

It is not a deity and does not possess human consciousness.  
Instead it functions as an instinctual regulatory structure maintaining balance between two cosmic energies.

- **Brighthaven**
- **Shadewoven**

When these energies remain balanced, reality remains stable.

When one force dominates, instability occurs.

The Concordance responds by influencing events through empowered agents known as **Arbiters**.

---

## Nature of the Concordance

The Concordance possesses:

- rudimentary awareness
- instinctive equilibrium responses
- indirect influence over reality

It does not command or dictate events directly.  
Instead it nudges probability, individuals, and circumstances toward restoring balance.

---

## The Scroll

Within the Concordance exists a dimension often described metaphorically as **The Scroll**.

Time within the Scroll behaves differently than in the mortal world.

Sections of the Scroll represent different eras of history.  
Arbiters can shift between these regions when necessary.

Older sections become increasingly unstable and dangerous.

One region in particular has become known as **The Storm**.
