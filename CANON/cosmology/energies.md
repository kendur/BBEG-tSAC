# Brighthaven and Shadewoven

Reality is sustained by the interaction of two opposing yet necessary forces.

## Brighthaven

Represents:

- order
- structure
- preservation
- stability

Brighthaven energy supports systems, institutions, and continuity.

Too much Brighthaven leads to rigid control and stagnation.

---

## Shadewoven

Represents:

- entropy
- transformation
- decay
- chaos

Shadewoven energy allows change, evolution, and destruction of failing systems.

Too much Shadewoven leads to instability and collapse.

---

## Balance

Neither force is inherently good or evil.

The Concordance exists to ensure equilibrium between them.

Arbiters are empowered with **both energies simultaneously**, allowing them to interact with the system without destabilizing it.
