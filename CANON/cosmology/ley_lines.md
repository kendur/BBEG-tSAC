# Ley Lines

Ley lines are the physical manifestation of the Concordance's energy lattice within the mortal world.

They form a planetary network through which Brighthaven and Shadewoven energies flow.

---

## Functions

Ley lines serve several purposes:

- channel cosmic energy
- anchor equilibrium in the physical world
- store the consciousness of individuals who significantly affected balance

---

## Consciousness in the Lattice

Individuals who influence equilibrium may eventually become part of the ley-line lattice.

Their consciousness does not vanish but becomes part of the system.

This state resembles an afterlife shaped by the individual's nature.

---

## Ley Line Scars

Major disruptions can damage the lattice.

These damaged regions are known as **Ley Line Scars**.

Effects include:

- hauntings
- supernatural phenomena
- mutated wildlife
- unstable energy fields

Many mortal legends originate from these locations.
