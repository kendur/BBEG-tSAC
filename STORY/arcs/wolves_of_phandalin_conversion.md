# Story Arc: Wolves of Phandalin Conversion

Notes on adapting the "Lost Mine of Phandelver" / "Wolves of Phandalin" scenario into the tSAC setting.

---

## Overview

This document tracks the conversion of the Wolves of Phandalin scenario into the tSAC framework. The goal is to use the scenario's structure and encounter beats as a vehicle for introducing players to the tSAC setting — grounding cosmic themes in a local, tangible conflict.

---

## Conversion Philosophy

The conversion should:

- Keep the **structural bones** of the original scenario (the journey, the missing contact, the local threat, the hidden dungeon)
- Replace **all cosmological framing** with tSAC equivalents — no D&D-specific lore
- Introduce tSAC mechanics and setting elements naturally through play
- Serve as an on-ramp to the larger Inversion arc without requiring players to know the full lore upfront

## Encounter Conversion Notes

### Opening (the road ambush)

The ambush becomes a Corrupted Guard patrol acting on intelligence about Solas's spiritlock. Their goal is capture, not killing — they want the spiritlock information.

### Phandalin / Ashford

The town is experiencing Inversion effects: unusual lost soul sightings, disturbing dreams connected to the spirit-thin place, and increasing Corrupted Guard pressure. The Veil Guard has a presence but is understaffed.

### Wave Echo Cave / Spiritlock Nexus

This is the emotional and thematic heart of the conversion. The nexus is the site of an ancient spiritlock that predates the current age. The Corrupted Guard wants access to it. So does the Veil Guard. What Solas finds there about their own spiritlock is a meaningful revelation.

---

## Mechanical Conversion

The conversion uses tSAC mechanics throughout:

- No D&D-specific spell lists, class abilities, or encounter design assumptions
- Challenge scaling uses Stamina/Willpower tension rather than HP/action economy
- Rest and recovery follow the tSAC injury model
- Supernatural elements (the nexus, any lost soul encounters) use full tSAC metaphysics
