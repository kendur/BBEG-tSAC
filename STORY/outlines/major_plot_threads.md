# Major Plot Threads

Active and planned major plot threads in the tSAC story.

---

## How to Use This Document

This document tracks the status and interrelationship of the major plot threads. It is updated as threads develop, are resolved, or are deprioritized. It is not a detailed outline — it is a map of what's in play.

---

## Active Threads

### Thread 1: The Inversion

**Status:** Active — primary arc  
**Summary:** The cosmic balance is failing. The Inversion is accelerating. All other threads feed into this one eventually.  
**Key open questions:**
- Can the Inversion be reversed, or only arrested?
- What would reversal require?
- Is the Betrayer the obstacle or, in some sense, a possible solution?

**See:** [Inversion of Light and Shadow](../arcs/inversion_of_light_and_shadow.md)

---

### Thread 2: Solas's Spiritlock

**Status:** Active — central mystery  
**Summary:** Solas carries an inherited spiritlock of unusual power and origin. Its connection to Protector-level events is unexplained. Uncovering the nature and origin of the spiritlock is a major story driver.  
**Key open questions:**
- Who created the original spiritlock?
- What is it bound to?
- Can it be used to affect the Inversion — and at what cost?

**See:** [Solas Aghrian](../../CANON/characters/solas_aghrian.md), [Spiritlock](../../CANON/metaphysics/spiritlock.md)

---

### Thread 3: The Betrayer's Agenda

**Status:** Active — mystery/antagonist thread  
**Summary:** The Betrayer is present and acting. Their true motivation is not yet revealed. The story requires that they be complex, not simply destructive.  
**Key open questions:**
- What did the Betrayer actually do, and why?
- What do they want now?
- What is the nature of their relationship to Solas's spiritlock?

**See:** [The Betrayer](../../CANON/characters/the_betrayer.md)

---

### Thread 4: The Veil Guard Crisis

**Status:** Active — institutional thread  
**Summary:** The Veil Guard is fracturing under the pressure of the Inversion. Their increasing authoritarianism is creating new enemies. The Corrupted Guard represents the extreme of where that path leads.  
**Key open questions:**
- Will the Veil Guard become an obstacle to solving the Inversion?
- Is there a reformable core, or has the institution itself become compromised?

**See:** [Veil Guard](../../CANON/factions/veil_guard.md), [Corrupted Guard](../../CANON/factions/corrupted_guard.md)

---

### Thread 5: Loughan Begarrow's Loyalty

**Status:** Active — character arc  
**Summary:** Loughan's pragmatism is both an asset and a potential breaking point. The story needs to force a choice that tests whether survival instinct has become moral abdication.  
**Key open questions:**
- What is the choice that defines Loughan's arc?
- Does Loughan's loyalty survive contact with the full truth of what the network knows and hasn't shared?

**See:** [Loughan Begarrow](../../CANON/characters/loughan_begarrow.md)
